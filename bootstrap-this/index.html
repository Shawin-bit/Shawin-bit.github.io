About Me

Hello, my name is Shawin, but I like to use my online alias name when designing random things. My Alias name is Skwesh or Skweshy. I’m a multi-media Designer who is currently enrolled in Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College. My Passion is Design and computers! The course I’m in, is prefect for my career goals. My goals in this field is to one day own a Design Company, providing people with graphic design, web development, and App Design. A personal Goal is to create an education app for specifically Cree Students to use, the purpose of this app would be to make learning Cree fun I have had many clients during the years of my studies, in one of my contracts i got to design and promote an event for 150 guests, i designed everything from the logo to the banners, bourcheres. It was a great experience. I have also Designed many posters. on my free time I do photography as a hobby. One of my Greatest skills is Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. I studied Life Drawing and Concept art at Algonquin College, I learnt how to draw scenes and models. So all around, I am a very open-minded and creative person! I hope you enjoy my website and my featured works!
